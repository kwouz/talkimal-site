Common questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

  • iOS: Settings → [Your Apple ID] → Subscriptions → Talkimal → Cancel.
  • Android: Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Talkimal → Cancel.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple or Google. Use:

  • Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Google: Google Play refund policy

Is the AI translation real?

No. Talkimal is an entertainment app. The AI-generated translations are creative interpretations, not real translations of animal communication. Talkimal is not a veterinary tool. If your pet shows signs of illness or distress, consult a licensed veterinarian.

Does the app work offline?

The recording feature requires an internet connection to process audio through our AI backend. You can browse and replay your saved history offline.

Is my audio stored on your servers?

No. Audio is uploaded for processing, sent to OpenAI’s API for transcription and analysis, then deleted from our infrastructure. The result is saved locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Which pets are supported?

Currently dogs, cats, birds, and guinea pigs. More species may be added in the future.

I lost my history after reinstalling. Can I recover it?

History is stored locally on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app. We do not currently sync history to the cloud.
